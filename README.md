# ClaimSense AI

## Intelligent Healthcare Revenue Cycle Management (RCM) Decision Support System Using Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG)

ClaimSense AI is an advanced AI-powered Revenue Cycle Management (RCM) intelligence platform designed to assist healthcare providers, hospitals, physician groups, and RCM organizations in optimizing claims management and reimbursement processes. The system leverages Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG), Natural Language Processing (NLP), Machine Learning, and Large Language Models (LLMs) to provide accurate, context-aware insights from healthcare claims documentation and RCM knowledge sources.

The platform enables users to upload and analyze healthcare-related documents such as payer policies, claim forms, denial letters, Explanation of Benefits (EOBs), coding guidelines, billing manuals, and standard operating procedures. By combining semantic search with generative AI, ClaimSense AI allows users to ask complex business and operational questions in natural language and receive precise answers grounded in the uploaded documentation.

A key capability of the system is its ability to recommend the **Next Best Action (NBA)** for a claim based on industry-standard RCM workflows, payer-specific guidelines, denial reasons, and historical knowledge. This assists revenue cycle teams in identifying the most appropriate corrective actions, reducing claim denials, accelerating reimbursement cycles, and improving overall collections performance.

### Key Benefits

#### For Hospitals and Healthcare Providers

* Maximizes reimbursement and revenue collection opportunities.
* Reduces claim processing delays and administrative burden.
* Improves claim resolution rates through intelligent decision support.
* Enhances operational efficiency across billing and collections teams.

#### For Physicians and Medical Practices

* Provides quick access to payer policies and billing guidelines.
* Assists in resolving complex claim and coding-related issues.
* Reduces revenue leakage caused by claim denials and documentation gaps.

#### For Revenue Cycle Management (RCM) Organizations

* Streamlines claim review and denial management workflows.
* Improves analyst productivity through AI-assisted decision-making.
* Standardizes operational processes across teams and clients.
* Enables faster turnaround time for claim adjudication and follow-up activities.

#### For RCM Analysts and Billing Specialists

* Delivers accurate answers to complex healthcare claims and reimbursement questions.
* Provides contextual recommendations aligned with established RCM standards and best practices.
* Reduces manual research effort by retrieving relevant information from large volumes of documents.
* Supports informed decision-making through intelligent document analysis and semantic search.

### Core Capabilities

* Healthcare document ingestion and analysis.
* Semantic search using vector embeddings and vector databases.
* Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) based question answering.
* Context-aware claim guidance and Next Best Action recommendations.
* Intelligent denial analysis and claim resolution support.
* Claim denial prediction using machine learning models.
* Query history and auditability.
* Analytics and reporting dashboard for operational insights.

ClaimSense AI serves as an intelligent decision-support platform that empowers healthcare organizations to improve financial performance, enhance operational efficiency, reduce claim denials, and make data-driven decisions throughout the Revenue Cycle Management lifecycle.
